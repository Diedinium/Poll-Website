Who should I vote for?

A quick answer: It's up to you.


Seriously, don't let others influence your decision. It should always be your own!


If there is ever a politically themed site that is telling you why you should be voting for a certain party, then it's either the parties main website, or is biased. That's the main message to leave this page with, based on the information about the parties above make your own informed decion about who you want to vote for. Have a think about the choice you make and see if any of these factors are influcencing your decision:

  • Your parents are voting for the one party, so you are as well.
  • The party you're voting for is certain to win in your area.
  • You're voting the same way as your friends.
  • You are worried about the repurcussions of voting for a certain party.
  • Someone is threatening you if you don't vote for a certain party.

If any of these are having an effect on the way you vote, then reconsider your choice, don't let others influence your vote unfairly, the decision needs to be your own. Remember...

Your vote, your choice!
