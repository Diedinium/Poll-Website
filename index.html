Welcome to Poll, the young persons voting information guide.

Voting amongst young people is the lowest among any age group, this website aims to change that.

Democracy only works when we all play our part, it may feel like you are only one person, and can't change much... this website is here to change that idea, and show you how powerful your vote is.
